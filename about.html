Skateboarding

Skateboarding: An Excellent Recreation to Become Good At!!!

I started skateboarding more often when the COVID-19 pandemic developed. With COVID-19, many of us were trapped indoors. I chose to skateboard because it would allow me to escape the indoors. Getting this fresh air was especially necessary after spending a couple ours on zoom in classes every day. Although I have always skateboarded, I started to skateboard more intensly during the past couple of months. Learning tricks on a skateboard was one of my goals. Although it takes many years to master many tricks, I was able to learn a couple over the summer.
